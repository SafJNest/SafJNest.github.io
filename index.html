information about the bot


Introducing Beebot - the ultimate Discord bot designed to enhance your server experience! With a wide range of features and functionalities, Beebot is here to make your Discord server more engaging and efficient. Whether you want to play music, access server and member statistics, track League of Legends stats, or manage your server effortlessly, Beebot has got you covered.
